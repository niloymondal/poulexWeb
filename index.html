About

MIMBA is a technological solution aimed at improving the dairy farming industry in Bangladesh. It offers a suite of advanced tools and services to help farmers better manage their livestock through real-time data and analytics. This includes unique animal identification via encrypted ear tags, health monitoring, and productivity tracking, all integrated into a user-friendly platform. MIMBA's technologies enable more efficient farm management, enhance animal welfare, and increase milk production, ultimately driving economic benefits for farmers and contributing to the modernization of the agriculture sector in Bangladesh.
